INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

Intellectual property rights is a term reffering to the legal property rights of a person over his her creations,both artistic and commercial

These laws ensure that the creator's hard work is safe and protected from unauthorised copying and piracy. The common types of Intellectual Property rights are
Industrial property
Industrial property includes trademark patent of innovation industrial design and geographical indication of source
Copyright
It is a right granted by law to the creator for an original work. Here the rights regarding creating a copy of any product are reserved by the author or the company.Legal action can be taken if someone copies work without permission of author Copyrights include artistic or litreary creations such as novels plays poem etc
