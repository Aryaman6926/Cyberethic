N E T I Q U E T T E S

The term netiquette is combination of two words Net(internet)and ettiquette (good manners) It is defined as special set of rulesthat governs behaviour of user communicating online The communication can be via email forums blog and networking sites You should adhere the same standards of behaviour online as you follow in real life



G U I D L I N E S   T O  F O L L O W  W H I L E  C O M M U N I C A T I N G  O N L I N E

Use proper salutation
While communicating online always begin your message with a salutation and end with signature

Include a subject line
give subjective phrase in the subject line of the message header that conveys purpose of message

Keep your message consice
Be precise and catchy while drafting message so that people read it eaisly

Do not indulge in spamming
Spamming reffers to flooding users inbox with unwanted spam mails or messages One should never respond to these messages

Avoid sarcasm and be respectful
Treat others like you would be treated by others Never include sarcastic or insultive comments in your message

Obey copyright law
Do not copy or post someones content from internet claiming your ownership always provide proper credits

Respect others piracy
do not seek into others email without their permission Going through other persons work could land you in trouble
